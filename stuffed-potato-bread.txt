Serving 2
Boil 3-4 potatoes
crush potatoes and add onions red chilli, turmeric, anardana, Garam masala
 salt green chillies chat masala

mix them well and 

roll wheat batter and fill it with above mixture. cover it using loose atta. 

again roll it and put on hot tawa . keep rotating so that it does not burn. 

once baked . apply some clarified cow butter. 

enjoy with curd or pickle.
